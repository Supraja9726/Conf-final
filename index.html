Rajalakshmi Engineering College is one of the leading NAAC and NBA accredited engineering colleges established under self-financing schemes in 1997. The college has been growing from strength to strength in the last 20 years.


About Rajalakshmi Engineering College:


Our college is moving ahead to become a Centre of Excellence in Engineering, Management Education, Research and Development. The College is located in serene atmosphere amidst many multinational companies in Thandalam, about 25 kilometers from Chennai on the Bangalore National Highway, providing the right industrial ambience for the budding engineers. The Institution currently offers 13 UG programs, 9 PG programs and 9 Research programs.


About CSE and IT department:


The Department offers 2 UG (B.E. CSE and B.Tech IT) and PG (M.E. CSE and M.E.Software Engineering) programs. The Departments were recognised as a Research centre by the Anna University. So far, 8 research scholars have obtained Ph.D. Degree and many more scholars are presently working in the centre. A large number of papers have been published in International journals by the department faculty members, PG students and research scholars.
Apart from the academic programs, students are motivated to participate in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities, encouraged to carry out innovative projects and take part in professional society activities. The Department has an active centre for Assistive Devices and Technologies, research for product development and dissemination which receive funded projects and consultancy amounts of 1.5 crores from various funding agencies like DST-TIDE, AICTE,ISRO,UGC,ISTE in the domain of assistive devices, automation and teaching tools.


About the Conference:


National Conference on Innovative Research Trends for Digital India is aimed to provide a forum for researchers in Academia and Industry to share their innovative thoughts and outcome of the research in latest technologies and development towards Digital India. This conference acts as a platform for both students and experienced professionals to know about the affordable outreach opportunities present in day-to-day life. The main aim of the conference is to kindle and bring out the latest research contributions towards the growth of the nation and to get benefited out of the knowlege gained. It covers the cutting edge technologies like Data Analytics, Internet of Things, Computer Vision and Networks in the context of Digital India to promote the nation to the next level.



Goals of the conference

* Expose the participants to the emerging technologies and methodoliges and make them more capable of solving tomorrow's challenges in a collaborative manner.
* Provide an enriched platform for Engineers, Technicians, Personnel from Government, Industry and Research organisations to share their vision, experience and perspective directly with Practitioners, Managers, Engineers, Students and Innovators.
